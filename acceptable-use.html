Acceptable Use Policy

Last Updated: May 2025

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") outlines the rules and guidelines for acceptable use of RayAPI's services. By using our Service, you agree to comply with this policy. Violations may result in suspension or termination of your account.

⚠️ Important: Violations of this policy may lead to immediate suspension of your account and forfeiture of any fees paid. We cooperate fully with law enforcement and regulatory authorities.

1. Prohibited Activities

You may not use RayAPI to create, generate, transmit, or facilitate content or activities that:

1.1 Illegal Content

  • Violate any applicable local, state, national, or international law
  • Facilitate illegal activities, including drug trafficking, human trafficking, or arms dealing
  • Promote or enable terrorism or extremist content
  • Generate false documents, fake IDs, counterfeit currency, or fraudulent certificates

1.2 Harmful Content

  • Generate content that is hateful, harassing, or discriminatory based on race, ethnicity, gender, religion, nationality, disability, sexual orientation, or other protected characteristics
  • Create content that exploits, harms, or threatens to harm minors
  • Generate violent, gore, or brutal content without legitimate journalistic, artistic, or educational purpose
  • Promote self-harm, suicide, or eating disorders

1.3 Weapons and Military Use

  • Develop, create, or disseminate information related to weapons of mass destruction or biological weapons
  • Generate content for military applications without proper authorization
  • Design weapons, explosives, or hazardous materials
  • Provide instructions for creating explosives or harmful devices

1.4 Deception and Fraud

  • Generate deceptive, misleading, or fraudulent content
  • Create fake reviews, testimonials, or endorsements
  • Impersonate real persons, organizations, or government entities
  • Generate synthetic media (deepfakes) without disclosure for deceptive purposes
  • Engage in phishing, social engineering, or scam operations

1.5 Privacy Violations

  • Collect personal information without consent
  • Generate content that invades privacy or discloses confidential information
  • Create content facilitating surveillance without authorization
  • Generate content for stalking, doxxing, or harassment campaigns

1.6 Malicious Activities

  • Generate malware, ransomware, viruses, or other malicious code
  • Create content for cybersecurity attacks, hacking, or unauthorized access
  • Develop tools for denial-of-service attacks
  • Generate content for spam or unsolicited communications

2. Prohibited Uses

2.1 Competing Services

You may not use RayAPI to:

  • Build competing AI API aggregation services
  • Reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble our Service
  • Scrape or extract data from our platform for competitive purposes
  • Circumvent rate limits or access restrictions

2.2 High-Risk Applications

Use of RayAPI for the following high-risk applications requires careful consideration and may require additional compliance measures:

  • Medical diagnosis, treatment, or health-related advice
  • Legal advice or representation
  • Financial investment recommendations
  • Critical infrastructure control systems
  • Decisions affecting employment, housing, credit, or essential services
Disclaimer: AI-generated content may contain errors or inaccuracies. For high-stakes decisions, human review and professional consultation are strongly recommended.

3. Export Controls and Sanctions Compliance

🚫 Sanctions Notice: RayAPI strictly prohibits use by individuals or entities located in, or affiliated with, countries subject to comprehensive U.S. or international sanctions.

You represent and warrant that you are not:

  • Located in, or a national of, a comprehensively sanctioned country (currently including Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, and the Crimea region of Ukraine)
  • Listed on the U.S. Treasury's OFAC Specially Designated Nationals (SDN) List or other restricted party lists
  • Owned or controlled by persons or entities from sanctioned countries

You agree not to use our Service for any end use prohibited by applicable export control laws, including:

  • Developing weapons or military applications
  • Surveillance technologies used for human rights abuses
  • Activities supporting nuclear proliferation or ballistic missile programs

4. Content Moderation

While RayAPI does not pre-screen all content, we implement automated systems and manual review to detect and prevent policy violations. We reserve the right to:

  • Monitor API usage patterns for compliance
  • Block or filter content that violates this policy
  • Suspend accounts suspected of violations pending investigation
  • Report illegal content to relevant authorities

5. Enforcement

5.1 Violation Consequences

If you violate this policy, we may take the following actions:

  • Warning: For minor or first-time violations
  • Content Blocking: Prevent generation of violating content
  • Rate Limiting: Temporarily restrict API access
  • Account Suspension: Temporarily disable account access
  • Account Termination: Permanently disable account and forfeit fees
  • Legal Action: Pursue damages and injunctive relief where appropriate

5.2 Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any violation of this policy, please report it immediately:

  • Email: abuse@rayapi.dev
  • Discord: Contact our moderation team

As a RayAPI user, you are responsible for:
